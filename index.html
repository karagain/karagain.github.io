About Me

I am a data scientist working at Reputation.com in the NLP group of the Rep Score team where we build models to transform text feedback from reviews and comments into an interpretable reputation score.
I will also soon be graduating from the Masters in Data Science (MSDS) at the University of San Francisco.

I have previous experience in AdTech, building recommendations and anomaly analysis tools, and consulting, for grassroot organizations.
